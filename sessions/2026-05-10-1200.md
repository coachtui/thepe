| Hybrid fill | 1,314 | 0.33 avg | **1,314** (all, warning severity) |

When selected hybrid items are evaluated with `evaluateSubmittalCoverageQA`, the
`low_extraction_confidence` finding fires with:
- severity: `info` (0.33 is in 0.50–0.79 range for most items... actually some are < 0.50)

Spot-check shows finding fires correctly: severity `info` for a 0.57-confidence fill item;
DD-form 0.92 item excluded.

Note: fill items cluster around 0.33–0.57 depending on missing fields. Items missing both
SD code and authority score 0.57 (0.72 − 0.10 − 0.05). Items with only missing SD code
score 0.62. Items missing both plus having very low base score lower than 0.30.

The `warning` threshold (< 0.50) would trigger for items where confidence was driven to 0.30
(the floor). Most fill items will be `info` range (0.50–0.79).
