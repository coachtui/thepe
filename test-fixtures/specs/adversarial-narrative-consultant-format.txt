SECTION 09 91 13 - EXTERIOR PAINTING

NOTE: This section was authored by the mechanical/electrical consultant and does not
follow standard CSI formatting. Submittal requirements are embedded in narrative prose.

GENERAL PROVISIONS

The painting contractor shall be responsible for all surface preparation, priming, and
topcoat application for all exterior surfaces as shown on the drawings and as specified
herein. The contractor shall submit product data sheets and color samples to the Architect
for review and approval prior to ordering any materials. SD-03 product data required for all
paint products. Prior to beginning work, contractor shall submit a complete color schedule
cross-referencing paint system, substrate, and color for each exterior surface type
(SD-03, Architect approval).

Paint mock-up panels shall be prepared in field locations as directed. Photographs of
accepted mock-up panels shall be submitted for record (SD-06, for information only).

WARRANTY SUBMITTALS: At project closeout, contractor shall submit manufacturer paint
warranty documents (SD-11 Closeout, no approval required) along with as-applied paint
records including batch numbers, application dates, and ambient conditions log.

SURFACE PREPARATION

All surfaces shall be clean, dry, and free of contaminants. Contractor shall submit a
written surface preparation and application procedure for review before beginning work.
This procedure submittal does not require Government approval — Architect review only.


SECTION 09 91 23 - INTERIOR PAINTING

Similar requirements apply for interior painting. The contractor must submit:

- Color selections: 3 physical samples of each proposed color per the color schedule.
  These are SD-08 samples and require Architect selection/approval.

- Product data for each paint product including VOC content, coverage, and application
  temperature range. Government approval required for all interior paints in food service
  areas. For other areas, Architect approval only. (SD-03)

- Applicator qualifications if the painting contractor has fewer than 5 years experience
  with commercial interior work. (SD-01, for information only)

The painting contractor shall also submit touch-up paint in labeled containers at
substantial completion — see Closeout requirements in Division 01. This is not a
formal submittal per se but shall be logged in the submittal register.
