## Known Limitations of First-Pass Parser

### False positive spec sections
Concatenated blobs like `01 14 00 01 20 00` can produce false positives like `14 00 01`
that match the CSI section pattern `\d{2}\s+\d{2}\s+\d{2}`. Root cause: two consecutive
sections share digits at the boundary. Observed: `14 00 01` (2 rows) and `05 52 00 06 41 16`
appear in the unique sections list as invalid combined entries.

Impact: minor ŌĆö a few rows have incorrect spec section attribution. SD codes are correct.

Fix for a future pass: use a CSI division allowlist (01-41 divisions for construction) or
require specific divison prefixes.

### Multi-section page attribution
Pages with multiple spec sections pair ALL SD codes with the FIRST spec section found.
This is approximate ŌĆö some SD codes belong to the second/third section.

### null spec sections
Some rows have `specSection: null` when the spec section blob is absent or unparseable.
