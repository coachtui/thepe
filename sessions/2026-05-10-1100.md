
The fill items (1314 narrative fill rows for 30 uncovered spec sections) have average
confidence 0.33 — significantly below the DD-form rows (0.92). This reflects the dual
penalty applied to rows missing both SD code and approval authority (which is common in
UFGS narrative body text).

The breakdown makes the quality split immediately visible: the 279 DD-form rows are
high-quality authoritative data; the 1314 fill rows are low-quality narrative fallback.
