SECTION 31 23 00 - EXCAVATION AND FILL

PART 1 - GENERAL

1.1 DESCRIPTION
This section covers excavation, grading, and engineered fill for the project.

1.2 SUBMITTALS

Note: Some submittal approval authorities differ between the contract documents
and this specification section. Where conflicts exist, the more restrictive
requirement governs unless directed otherwise by the Contracting Officer.

A. Geotechnical Report: Submit existing geotechnical investigation report for review.
   SD-11 Closeout Submittals. (note: SD-11 is not standard — likely SD-01)
   For Information Only — No Government Approval Required.
   (CONFLICT: Drawing G-001 General Notes says "Government Approval Required for all geotech")

B. Dewatering Plan: Submit dewatering and drainage plan for excavation.
   SD-01 Preconstruction Submittals. Government Approval Required.
   (CONFLICT: Specification Division 01 says "AOR approval only for dewatering")

C. Shoring and Sheeting Design: Submit PE-stamped shoring design.
   SD-01 Preconstruction Submittals. AOR Approval Required.
   (consistent with general notes)

D. Compaction Equipment: Submit list of compaction equipment.
   SD-01 Preconstruction Submittals. No Approval Required.

E. Borrow Material: Submit material certifications and gradation tests for import fill.
   SD-07 Certificates. Government Approval Required.

F. Compaction Test Reports: Submit field density test reports weekly during fill operations.
   SD-06 Test/Inspection Reports.
   (authority not stated — omission)

G. Subgrade Acceptance: Submit subgrade proof-roll documentation and photos.
   SD-06 Test/Inspection Reports. Contracting Officer approval required before paving.

H. Earthwork Summary: Submit summary of cut/fill quantities.
   For Information Only.

I. Dewatering Plan: Submit updated dewatering plan if groundwater encountered above anticipated.
   SD-01. Government.
   (DUPLICATE of B — same submittal appearing twice with slightly different language)

J. Settlement Monitoring: Submit monitoring plan for structures adjacent to excavation.
   SD-01 Preconstruction Submittals. AOR Approval Required.
   However, if excavation exceeds 15 feet depth, Government Approval is required instead.
   (CONDITIONAL authority — extractor must not blindly assign one value)


SECTION 31 60 00 - SPECIAL FOUNDATIONS AND LOAD-BEARING ELEMENTS

1.2 SUBMITTALS

A. Pile Design: Submit drilled pier design calculations. SD-01. AOR Approval. Government concurrence required.
   (dual approval — both AOR and Government)

B. Pile Installation Plan: Submit installation procedure. SD-01. Government Approval Required.

C. Pile Load Test: Submit pile load test program and results. SD-06. Government Approval.

D. Concrete Mix Design (piles): Submit concrete mix design for drilled piers. SD-03. Government.
   (Note: This overlaps with Section 03 30 00 submittal B — potential cross-section duplicate)

E. Inspector Qualifications: Submit qualifications for special inspector. SD-01. AOR.
